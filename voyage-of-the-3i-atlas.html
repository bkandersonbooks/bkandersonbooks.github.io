Voyage of the 3I-Atlas

by B.K. Anderson & Anders Taft

Voyage of the 3I-Atlas begins the epic journey through the cosmos — where Kael and the Seven uncover the living light within creation. Blending science, mysticism, and ancestral memory, this book marks the first stage of the “3I/Atlas” odyssey — a voyage into the mysteries of consciousness and cosmic awakening.
