About This Book

In The City of the Seventh Heart, the long journey of restoration reaches its fulfillment. The seven bogs have awakened. The seven cities breathe once more beneath the surface of Mars. The hum that began as a whisper now resonates across an entire world.

Asa and Saxifraga stand at the center of a transformation greater than either imagined. As the Seventh Heart awakens, seven children of resonance emerge—one for each city. Their presence sends a call beyond the cities, beyond the bogs, and beyond memory itself.

Across the stars, the seven ancient races begin to hear what they thought was lost forever: the voice of home. What follows is not an ending, but the beginning of a new chapter in the story of Mars, belonging, and life itself.

The final volume of the Seven Cities of Mars explores unity, remembrance, healing, and the quiet realization that everything is connected through a single living heart.
