Series Overview

Mars is not dead—only sleeping. Beneath seven ancient wetlands lie seven buried cities, each tied to a lesson of renewal and guarded by an ancient presence that listens for the right tone. As Saxifraga and Asa move from bog to bog, the restoration of a world becomes not only an act of healing, but a journey toward belonging, awakening, and the return of life itself.
