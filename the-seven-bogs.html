Series Overview

Mars is not dead—only sleeping. Beneath seven ancient wetlands lie seven subterranean cities, each guarded by a One-of-One that listens for the right tone. When the planetary hum falters, Saxifraga awakens—and Asa, a beekeeper, becomes the living bridge that can help a world remember how to breathe.
