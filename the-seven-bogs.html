The Seven Bogs of Mars

A world that remembers. A guardian who awakens. A human who learns to listen.

Mars was never meant to be silent.

Beneath its ancient surface lie the Seven Bogs — living ecosystems created long ago to sustain life across a changing world. Each Bog sleeps beneath the dust, guarded by a dormant intelligence known as a One-of-One.

When a human beekeeper named Asa arrives on Mars, something unexpected happens. The bees carry a resonance the Bog recognizes. Slowly, quietly, life begins to stir again.

But awakening one Bog is only the beginning.

Seven must rise before Mars can truly live again.
