The Seven Bogs of Mars

A world that remembers. A guardian who awakens. A human who learns to listen.

Mars was never meant to be silent.

Beneath its surface lie the Seven Bogs—living ecosystems built long ago to hold life in trust. Each one sleeps, guarded by a One-of-One.

When Asa and his bees become part of Mars, the first Bog begins to answer. And once one stirs… the others start to call.
