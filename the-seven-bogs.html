About This Book

Mars was never truly dead. Beneath its ancient surface, hidden from human eyes, seven living bogs waited in silence. Their purpose forgotten, their voices fading, they remained dormant for ages beyond memory.

When Asa is drawn into a mystery larger than himself, he discovers that life on Mars is not measured by machines or cities, but by living ecosystems that remember a forgotten harmony. Alongside Saxifraga and the bees that carry the first signs of renewal, he begins a journey that will awaken worlds, restore belonging, and reveal the deeper connection between humanity and life itself.

The Seven Bogs of Mars begins an epic journey of restoration, remembrance, and hope that spans worlds, generations, and the hidden heart of Mars.
