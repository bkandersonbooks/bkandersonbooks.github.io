About This Book

In the final volume of The Seven Bogs of Mars, the long journey reaches its destination. The lessons of Patience, Silence, Movement, Restraint, Passage, and Interconnection now converge within the Seventh Heart. What began as the healing of a single bog becomes the awakening of an entire world.

Asa and Saxifraga stand together at the center of a living Mars transformed by belonging. The bees have carried life between the bogs, the cities have begun to awaken, and the resonance of the One-of-One can once again be heard. Yet the greatest discovery is not found beneath Mars. It is found within the heart of every living thing.

The Bog of the Seventh Heart is a story of remembrance, unity, and hope—a quiet reflection on what becomes possible when life chooses connection over separation and belonging over fear.
