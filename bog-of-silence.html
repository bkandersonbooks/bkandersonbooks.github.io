About This Book

In The Bog of Silence, Asa discovers that not every wounded place can be healed by movement, work, or force. Some places must first become quiet enough to hear what still lives beneath them.

As the ancient echoes fade, the soft hum of the bees becomes the first living tone the Bog has heard in ages. Within that silence, the Listener begins to awaken.
