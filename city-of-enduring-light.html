The City of Enduring Light

Book One · The Seven Cities of Mars

← Back to Series Home

Beneath the Martian surface, the first city begins to awaken. What was once silent stone now carries a steady light—ancient, patient, and waiting to be remembered. As Asa and Saxifraga step beyond the restored bogs, they encounter a presence unlike anything before: a civilization that does not speak, but endures. This is not a place built on power, but on constancy. The City of Enduring Light holds the first key to humanity’s return—not through conquest, but through alignment. Here, movement is not required. Only presence. As the city stirs, Asa must learn that not all awakening is sudden. Some forms of life return slowly… and ask the same in return. The journey continues—but the path forward begins with stillness.
