The Listeners

Book Two of The Seven Races
by Brian Keith Anderson

About the Book

Beneath the restored City of Silence, a deeper wisdom begins to awaken. The second race has heard the quiet call carried through the living resonance of Mars, the bees, the bogs, the children, and the city itself.

The Listeners are the race of silence, wisdom, and deep understanding. They do not return with noise or command, but with stillness. They have waited across the ages until Mars was quiet enough to hear them again.

As Asa, Saxifraga, and the children witness their return, humanity begins to understand that not all answers arrive as words. Some truths can only be received when the heart becomes still enough to listen.

This is the second race returning home.
