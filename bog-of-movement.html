About This Book

In The Bog of Movement, Asa and Saxifraga enter a living world that has grown still for too long. Water must move, bees must carry life forward, and the bog must remember that survival is not found in stillness alone, but in the courage to flow again.

As Asa walks the paths of the bog and learns its hidden rhythms, he begins to understand that movement is more than travel. It is trust. It is change. It is the willingness to keep going even when the way ahead is uncertain.
