About This Book

In The City of Flowing Currents, Asa and Saxifraga enter the third awakened city beneath Mars, a place shaped by movement, balance, and the living rhythm of water, light, bees, and memory.

After the lessons of endurance and silence, the city teaches a new truth: life cannot remain still forever. What has been restored must now begin to move. Currents must flow. Pathways must open. The living world beneath Mars must learn how to breathe through motion again.

As Asa follows the currents through the city, he begins to understand that movement is not escape. It is trust. It is the willingness to let life carry what still belongs to the whole.
