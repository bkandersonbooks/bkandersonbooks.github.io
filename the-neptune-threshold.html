About This Book

As the 3I-ATLAS reaches the distant realm of Neptune, the crew finds themselves standing at the edge of humanity's greatest discovery. Strange signals, unexpected allies, and mysteries hidden beyond the blue giant reveal that the Solar System is far more alive than anyone imagined. As Kael's understanding of consciousness continues to evolve, the crew must confront questions that blur the line between science, intelligence, and spiritual awakening. The Neptune Threshold is a journey of discovery, courage, and wonder that leads directly toward humanity's next great horizon.
