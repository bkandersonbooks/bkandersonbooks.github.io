The Roberts Family Legacy

From Cedar Creek to Tate Cemetery — by B.K. Anderson

The Roberts Family Legacy follows the ancestral journey from Cedar Creek, Virginia, through Boiling Springs Creek, Cowan, Tennessee, onward to Battle Creek Road in Marion County, and finally to Tate Cemetery. Each place marks a generation’s passage, bound by endurance, love, and faith. What began on Virginia soil continues in Tennessee stone—stories rooted in both remembrance and Light.
