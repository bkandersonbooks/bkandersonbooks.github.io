About This Book

In The Bog of Restraint, Asa and Saxifraga arrive at a bog that has forgotten balance. Growth once flourished here, but without wisdom it became excess. The land must now learn that life is not measured by how much it takes, but by how well it lives within its limits.

As the bees continue their work and the bog slowly awakens, Asa discovers that restraint is not weakness. It is the quiet strength that allows life to endure. Through patience, understanding, and measured action, the bog begins to heal and remember its true purpose.
