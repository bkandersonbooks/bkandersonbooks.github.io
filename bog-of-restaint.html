About This Book

Asa enters a bog where life has not failed from weakness, but from excess. Growth has pushed too far, movement has become strain, and the living systems of Mars now require a different lesson: restraint.

With Saxifraga beside him and the bees carrying the quiet rhythm of restoration, Asa must learn that healing does not always come through action. Sometimes life is saved by holding back, listening longer, and allowing balance to return before the next step is taken.

The Bog of Restraint continues the Seven Bogs of Mars series, deepening Asa’s journey through patience, silence, movement, and now the sacred discipline of balance.
