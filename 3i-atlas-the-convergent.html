About This Book

The Convergent is the moment the sky becomes a mirror. As 3I-Atlas nears Jupiter, observation, prediction, and public belief collide—forcing Kael Andersson to stand between what can be measured and what can only be witnessed. Models shift. Narratives fracture. And in the tightening window of alignment, the question becomes unavoidable: is 3I-Atlas merely passing through… or arriving?
