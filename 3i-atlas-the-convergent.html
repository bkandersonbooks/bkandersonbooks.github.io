3I-ATLAS: The Convergent

3I-ATLAS Series — Book IV

About This Book

The Convergent follows the 3I-ATLAS story into a new phase—when observation, interpretation, and public reaction begin to overlap. As the object approaches the outer planets, strange geometries, unexpected behavior, and competing narratives push the world into a single question: what, exactly, are we witnessing?

This volume continues the 3I-ATLAS record through three layers: the observer’s log, the scientific meaning of what’s seen, and the human response as attention intensifies.
