The Enduring Ones

Book One of The Seven Races
by Brian Keith Anderson

About the Book

Beneath the restored City of Enduring Light, something ancient begins to stir. The first of the seven races has heard the call, carried through the living resonance of the city, the bog, the bees, and the children born to bridge what was lost with what is returning.

The Enduring Ones are not conquerors, rulers, or gods. They are the race of patience, memory, and deep-rooted strength — beings who waited until the city was ready to receive them again.

As Asa, Saxifraga, and the children witness the first signs of their return, humanity must begin to understand that survival is not found in control, but in belonging.

This is the beginning of the Seven Races returning home.
