About This Book

In The Last Ancestor, Rowan stands on the threshold of his own seven-day vision quest. Before he takes his first step into the unknown, his father, Asa, asks him to sit beside the ancient stone circle and listen. What follows is the story of an ordinary life that became something far greater than either of them ever imagined.

As Asa looks back across the Seven Bogs, the hidden Cities, and the return of the Seven Races, forgotten moments take on new meaning. Every choice, every friendship, every loss, and every small act of hope has been leading toward a single purpose woven through generations of ancestors and carried in the memory of Mars itself.

Rich with wonder, mystery, and quiet wisdom, The Last Ancestor brings together the bees, the honey jar, Saxifraga, the Seven Hearts, and the living spirit of an ancient world in a deeply satisfying conclusion. Yet as one story comes to an end, another is only beginning, for Rowan must soon face the Gates that await him beyond the circle.
