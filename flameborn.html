The Flameborn

Book Three of The Seven Races
by Brian Keith Anderson

About the Book

The City of Becoming Flame has awakened beneath Mars, sending its living resonance across the stars to those who once carried its fire within them.

The Flameborn are the race of emergence, transformation, and renewal. Long ago they left Mars seeking knowledge, power, and purpose. Yet through the centuries they forgot that the greatest transformation is not becoming something new—it is remembering who you truly are.

As Asa, Saxifraga, and the children witness their return, the city reveals a deeper lesson. Fire can destroy, but it can also illuminate. What matters is the heart that guides it.

The Flameborn must learn that true emergence is not rising above others, but rising into harmony with life itself as the third race answers the call of Mars.
