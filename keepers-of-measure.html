The Keepers of Measure

Book Four of The Seven Races
by Brian Keith Anderson

About the Book

Deep beneath Mars, the City of the Measured Hand awakens, sending a quiet resonance across the stars. Those who hear it know the call is meant for them.

The Keepers of Measure are the race of restraint, balance, and wisdom. Long ago they believed harmony could be achieved through control. Over time they learned that life cannot be forced into balance—it must be lived into balance.

As Asa, Saxifraga, and the children witness their return, they discover that every living thing carries its own rhythm. The bees, the bogs, the cities, and the races all flourish when given room to become what they were meant to be.

The Keepers of Measure must remember that true restraint is not limitation. It is the wisdom to know when to act, when to wait, and when to trust life to find its own way as the fourth race returns home to Mars.
