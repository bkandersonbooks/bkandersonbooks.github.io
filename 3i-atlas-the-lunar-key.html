Voyage of the 3I-Atlas

3I-Atlas Series — Book One

About This Book

*Voyage of the 3I-Atlas* launches a visionary journey beyond the stars and into the depths of consciousness. Co-authored by B.K. Anderson and Anders Taft, this first entry in the *3I-Atlas Series* follows the awakening of explorers who uncover messages encoded in cosmic light. Blending science-fiction, mysticism, and human awakening, it opens the gateway to a new era of discovery — where spirit and science meet among the stars.
