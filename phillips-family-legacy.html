About this Book

This volume celebrates the enduring connection between the Phillips and Anderson families — two lineages joined through love, perseverance, and faith. Within these pages, their stories, photographs, and remembrances weave together a portrait of unity that spans generations. Through this work, their shared legacy continues to speak, reminding all who follow of the strength born from family and the grace of memory preserved in Light.
