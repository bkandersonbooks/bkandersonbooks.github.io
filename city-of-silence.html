About This Book

In The City of Silence, Asa and Saxifraga journey into a place unlike any they have encountered before. Hidden beneath the surface of Mars lies a city that has waited through ages of stillness, preserving a wisdom that can only be found when noise finally fades away.

As the city slowly awakens, Asa discovers that silence is not emptiness. It is presence. It is listening deeply enough to hear what has always been there. Ancient pathways, forgotten chambers, and the subtle hum of life reveal a lesson that cannot be taught through words alone.

At the heart of the city rests the Listener, a One-of-One whose awakening depends not on power or force, but on true quiet. Through bees, resonance, and patience, Asa begins to understand that wisdom often arrives only after we stop trying to control what we hear.

The second volume of the Seven Cities of Mars explores stillness, listening, inner awareness, and the quiet strength that emerges when life is allowed to speak for itself.
