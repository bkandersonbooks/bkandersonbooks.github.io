The Weavers

Book Six of The Seven Races
by Brian Keith Anderson

About the Book

The City of Interconnection has awakened beneath Mars, sending a living resonance through every city, every bog, and every path that joins one life to another.

The Weavers are the race of connection, belonging, and shared purpose. Long ago they understood that all life is linked together, but over the centuries they began to see themselves as separate from the living web they once served.

As Asa, Saxifraga, and the children witness their return, they discover a truth reflected everywhere around them. The bees belong to the bogs. The bogs belong to the cities. The cities belong to the races. And the races belong to something greater than themselves.

The Weavers must remember that no life stands alone. Every choice becomes a thread, every act becomes part of a larger pattern, and every heart contributes to the whole. As the sixth race returns home to Mars, the ancient web of belonging begins to awaken once more.
