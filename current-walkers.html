The Current Walkers

Book Two of The Seven Races
by Brian Keith Anderson

About the Book

Deep beneath Mars, the City of Flowing Currents has awakened. Through its living resonance, a call spreads outward into the stars, reaching those who once left their home behind.

The Current Walkers are the race of movement, change, and adaptation. For generations they wandered far from Mars, believing that progress meant moving onward. Yet the city's song reminds them that true movement is not escape—it is returning to the place where belonging began.

As Asa, Saxifraga, and the children witness their arrival, the lessons of the living bogs deepen. Life must move or it dies, but movement without purpose becomes wandering.

The Current Walkers must rediscover the balance between journey and home as the second race answers the call of Mars.
