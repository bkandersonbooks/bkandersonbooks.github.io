About This Book

In The City of Restraint, Asa and Saxifraga arrive at a city unlike any they have encountered before. Here, every structure, pathway, and garden reflects a deeper lesson: growth without balance eventually destroys itself.

Within the ancient halls of the city, Asa encounters the wisdom of the Keepers of Measure, guardians who once understood that strength is not found in taking more, but in knowing when enough has been reached. Through their legacy, he learns that restraint is not limitation—it is harmony.

As the city slowly awakens, Asa discovers that life flourishes not through endless expansion but through careful stewardship, patience, and balance. The lesson of restraint becomes one of the most difficult—and most important—teachings on the path toward restoring Mars.
