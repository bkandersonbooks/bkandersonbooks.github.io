The Gatekeepers

Book Five of The Seven Races
by Brian Keith Anderson

About the Book

The City of the Open Gate has awakened beneath Mars, and its resonance reaches farther than any city before it. Across distant worlds, an ancient race hears the call and begins the journey home.

The Gatekeepers are the race of passage, trust, and welcome. For generations they guarded the thresholds between places, peoples, and possibilities. Yet over time they forgot that a gate exists not only to protect, but also to open.

As Asa, Saxifraga, and the children witness their return, they discover that every path requires courage. Some gates lead outward into discovery, while others lead inward toward understanding and belonging.

The Gatekeepers must learn that wisdom is not found in keeping every door closed. It is found in knowing when to open the way, when to welcome others, and when to trust life enough to let it pass through as the fifth race returns home to Mars.
