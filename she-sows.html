She Sows

The Bog Series • Book Three
by B.K. Anderson
The cycle completes.

What was awakened and sought must now be given back. The living bog no longer waits—it responds, it remembers, and it becomes.

Saxifraga stands at the threshold of a deeper truth: restoration is not control.

It is trust.

As the final rhythms unfold, she learns that to restore life is to release it— to sow it forward into a future she may never fully see.

And in that quiet act, something new begins.
